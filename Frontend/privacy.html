Privacy Policy

Data Security and Usage

We believe in keeping your proprietary models and datasets private. The data you process is encrypted sequentially. At no point is your CSV tabular data or PDF documentation utilized to publicly train broader foundational models without specific opt-in actions.

Information Gathering

We log interactions purely for error-correction limits and caching strategies. Auth credentials and connection strings are securely hashed over HTTPS connections and never stored in plain-text arrays.

Third-Party Integrations

Utilizing the Google Gemini API requires forwarding certain queries downstream to process the generative AI output. We recommend visiting Google's Vertex AI security standards for further transparency on multi-tenant execution constraints.
